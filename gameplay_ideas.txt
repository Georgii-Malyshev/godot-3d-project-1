+ Mana slowly regenerates, but only up to a certain low threshold and only if it was not spent in the last few seconds
  - mana regeneration can be altered by some dolmens but with a downside

+ Health slowly regenerates, but only up to a very low threshold (about 15% of max?)
  and only if no damage was taken in the last few seconds;
  - health regeneration can be altered by some dolmens but with a downside

- Stats are increased by finding certain items, base damage stays the same

- Some areas are dangerous to stand immobile in (enemies can find you,
  in the swamp you start to slowly sink, in the darkness things start nibbing at your health etc.)
